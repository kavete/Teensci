Main engineering disciples

Engineering is a broad field that is divided into various disciplines and sub-disciplines. The disciples of Engineering can be divided into four main categories;

  • ✔Chemical Engineering
  • ✔Civil Engineering
  • ✔Electrical Engineering
  • ✔Mechanical Engineering

Each one of the disciplines also has it's sub-disciplines. For instance mechanical engineering can constitute of Aerospace engineering and Automotive engineering among others.

Mechanical Engineering as a discipline

Mechanical engineering is one of the broadest disciplines in engineering.
Mechanical engineering is the application of the principles and problem solving techniques of engineering starting from the design to the manufacturing.

There are several engineering fields under Mechanical engineering such as Statics, Material science, Thermodynamics, Fluid mechanics, manufacturing, machine design and so much more.
Main sub-disciplines under Mechanical engineering include;

  • ✔Automotive engineering
  • ✔Aerospace engineering
  • ✔Aeronautical engineering
  • ✔Robotics engineering
  • ✔Mechatronic engineering
  • ✔Biomedical engineering
  • ✔Manufacturing engineering
  • ✔Acoustical engineering
  • ✔Marine engineering

Electrical engineering discipline

Electrical engineering deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism.
Electrical engineering involves power, optoelectronics, digital electronics, computer science, Artificial intelligence, control systems, electronics, signal processing, telecommunications and many more.

The main sub disciplines include;

  • ✔Electronics engineering
  • ✔Telecommunications engineering
  • ✔Communications engineering
  • ✔Computer engineering
  • ✔Power and energy engineering
  • ✔Nuclear engineering
  • ✔Satellite engineering
  • ✔Signal processing

Civil engineering discipline

Civil engineering is one of the oldest engineering disciplines. There are so many ancient structures designed and built by people whom we can call civil engineers. It deals with the design, construction and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, structural components of buildings and much more.
The main fields include; construction, forensics, geotechnical, structural, surveying, transport, water resources and so much more.

The main sub-disciplines include

  • ✔Structural engineering
  • ✔Architectural engineering
  • ✔Transportation engineering
  • ✔Construction engineering
  • ✔Geotechnical engineering
  • ✔Environmental engineering
  • ✔Hydraulic engineering

Chemical engineering discipline

Chemical engineering deals with the transformation of raw materials into useful, innovative products for public and commercial consumption.
Chemical engineering majorly deals with ceramics, materials, petroleum, plastics and more.

The major sub disciplines under chemical engineering include;

  • ✔Petroleum engineering
  • ✔Production engineering
  • ✔Process engineering and design
  • ✔Reservoir engineering
  • ✔Material engineering
  • ✔Biochemical engineering
